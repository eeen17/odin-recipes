The most class, easy, and delicious bread in the world. No kneading, just pure, unadulterated bread. Let's go make some bread.

Ingredients

  • Water - 220g
  • Yeast - 5g
  • Sugar - 2g
  • Bread flour - 250g
  • Salt - 3g
  • Olive Oil - 8g
Toppings
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Anything else you want!

Prep

  1. Mix the water, yeast, and sugar.
    • It's okay if there are tiny clumps of sugar! This is just a little meal for the yeast to do its job.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix the flour and salt.
  3. Slowly incorporate the wet mixture into the dry. Mix until it becomes a dough.
  4. Add olive oil and mix.
  5. Let it sit at room temperature for 40 minutes.
  6. Now, use your hands and stretch the bottom of the dough over to the top. Do this for a couple minutes.
  7. Repeat the last 2 steps once more.
If you are going to bake at a different time, put the dough in the fridge. Then before baking, let it rest at room temp for half an hr to a full hr.

Bake

  1. Add topping if you would like at this stage.
  2. Flick a small amount of water so that it's evenly distributed over the surface.
  3. Preheat the oven to 440Â°F, and bake for 15 minutes.
Slice and Enjoy!
